How to Play

Blackjack Rules

🎯 Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over. If your hand exceeds 21, you "bust" and lose automatically.

🃏 Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) — face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) — worth 10
  • Ace (A) — worth 11, or 1 if 11 would bust

🏆 Payouts

🥇 Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first 2 cards) — pays 3 to 2
✅ Regular Win — pays 1 to 1
🤝 Push (tie with dealer) — bet returned
🛡️ Insurance Win — pays 2 to 1

🎮 Your Actions

  • Hit — take another card
  • Stand — keep your hand, end your turn
  • Double Down — double your bet, take exactly one more card
  • Split — split two equal-value cards into two hands (costs an extra bet)
  • Surrender — forfeit the hand, recover half your bet

🏦 Dealer Rules

  • Dealer must draw cards until reaching 17 or higher
  • Dealer must stand on 17 (including soft 17)
  • Dealer's second card is face-down until your turn ends
  • If dealer busts, all remaining player hands win

🛡️ Insurance

Offered when the dealer shows an Ace. You may bet up to half your original bet. If the dealer has Blackjack, insurance pays 2 to 1. If not, insurance is lost.
